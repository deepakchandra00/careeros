- CREATED `/home/z/my-project/src/lib/resume/section-splitter.ts`
  - Pure-regex 6-section splitter (no AI, instant). Exports `splitResumeIntoSections(text)` → `{contact, summary, skills, experience, projects, education}`.
  - 5 case-insensitive anchored header regex patterns per section. Contact = everything before first header. Each section body spans header→next header (or end). Decorative underlines (lines of only `-=_*~`) skipped. Lines > 80 chars not treated as headers.
  - Header patterns cover all variants from spec: Professional Summary/Summary/Profile/Objective/About Me/Career Summary/Career Objective/Professional Profile/Executive Summary; Technical Skills/Skills/Key Skills/Core Competencies/Core Skills/Tech Stack/Areas of Expertise/Relevant Skills/Technology Stack/Technologies; Professional Experience/Work Experience/Experience/Employment/Employment History/Work History/Career History/Professional Background/Career Experience; Key Projects/Projects/Notable Projects/Personal Projects/Side Projects/Selected Projects/Signature Projects; Education/Academic/Qualifications/Education & Certifications/Education and Certifications/Academic Background/Academic Qualifications. All optional trailing colon.
  - Skills compression: strips pure category header lines (`^[A-Z][\w\s&/,\-]{0,60}:\s*$`), strips leading "Category:" prefixes from mixed lines, splits on `, ; | •`, dedupes case-insensitively (keeps first-seen casing), joins with ", ". Saves ~1,000 chars.
  - Also exports `splitIntoChunks(text, targetChar)` → string[]: splits at paragraph boundaries (double newlines); hard-splits at word boundaries for paragraphs that themselves exceed targetChar. Used for sub-chunking large experience/projects sections.
